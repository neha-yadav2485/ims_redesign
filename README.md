## What's New

The original site felt dated and hard to navigate. This redesign focuses on three things: **visual credibility**, **information clarity**, and **lead conversion**.

- **Hero section** with animated floating cards and key stats
- **Course cards** with colour-coded tags for CAT, CLAT, GATE, GMAT, GRE, SAT, IELTS, CUET
- **Why IMS section** highlighting the institute's key strengths
- **Animated stat counters** (75K+ students, 150+ centres, 40 years)
- **Mentor grid** with photos, roles, and bios for all Delhi faculty
- **Testimonials** from real alumni
- **All 12 Delhi NCR centres** with addresses, phone numbers, and map links
- **FAQ accordion** covering common student queries
- **Lead capture CTA** with inline form
- Sticky navbar that shrinks on scroll
- Fully **responsive** across mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Zero external dependencies** — just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS (+ Google Fonts)

## Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (custom properties, grid, flexbox, animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Google Fonts — [Syne](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Syne) + [DM Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans)

No frameworks. No build tools. Just open `index.html` in a browser.

## Design Decisions

| Choice | Reasoning |
|--------|-----------|
| Warm parchment background (`#f5f2eb`) | Feels premium and academic, avoids the cold sterile look of most coaching sites |
| Gold accent (`#c8972a`) | Signals excellence and legacy without being flashy |
| Syne typeface | Bold, geometric — memorable and modern without being trendy |
| Dark "Why IMS" section | Creates visual contrast and makes the value props feel more authoritative |
| Inline single-file build | Zero setup friction — anyone can open and share it instantly |
